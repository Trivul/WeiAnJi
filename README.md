胃安记隐私政策

胃安记是一款本地健康记录工具。应用用于记录症状、饮食、用药、睡眠、月经周期和相关健康笔记。

我们不会收集、上传、出售或共享用户的个人数据。用户输入的记录默认仅保存在用户自己的设备本地。

如果用户删除应用，相关本地数据可能会随应用一并删除。用户可以自行导出或备份记录。

本应用不包含广告追踪，不会将数据发送给第三方分析或广告平台。

WeiAnJi Privacy Policy

WeiAnJi is a local health tracking tool. The app is used to record symptoms, diet, medications, sleep, menstrual cycles, and related health notes.

We do not collect, upload, sell, or share users' personal data. User-entered records are saved locally on the user's own device by default.

If a user deletes the app, the related local data may be deleted along with the app. Users can export or back up their records themselves.

This app does not contain ad tracking and does not send data to third-party analytics or advertising platforms.
