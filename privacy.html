1. 应用说明

安愈记是一款本地健康记录工具，帮助用户记录身体不适、饮食、用药、睡眠、月经周期和日常健康变化。

2. 数据收集

安愈记不会主动收集、上传、出售或共享用户的个人数据。用户在应用中输入的记录默认仅保存在用户自己的设备本地。

3. 本地存储

用户输入的症状、饮食、用药、睡眠和其他健康记录会存储在本机设备中。删除应用可能会导致本地数据被删除，请在删除应用前自行备份重要记录。

4. 第三方服务

安愈记目前不包含广告追踪，不会将用户数据发送给第三方广告平台或分析平台。

5. 医疗免责声明

安愈记仅用于个人健康记录和自我管理参考，不提供医学诊断、治疗建议或处方建议。如有严重、持续或突发症状，请及时咨询医生或合格医疗专业人员。

1. About the App

MyWellLog is an offline health diary that helps users record chronic discomfort, diet, medication, sleep, menstrual cycle, and daily health changes.

2. Data Collection

MyWellLog does not actively collect, upload, sell, or share users’ personal data. Information entered in the app is stored locally on the user’s own device by default.

3. Local Storage

Symptoms, meals, medication, sleep, and other health records entered by users are stored on the local device. Deleting the app may also remove locally stored data, so users should back up important information before uninstalling the app.

4. Third-Party Services

MyWellLog currently does not include ad tracking and does not send user data to third-party advertising or analytics platforms.

5. Medical Disclaimer

MyWellLog is intended only for personal health recording and self-management reference. It does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment advice, or prescriptions. If you experience severe, persistent, or sudden symptoms, please consult a doctor or qualified healthcare professional promptly.
