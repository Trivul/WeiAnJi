常见问题

1. 胃安记的数据会上传到服务器吗？
不会。胃安记默认将用户记录保存在本机设备中。

2. 删除 App 后数据还会保留吗？
删除 App 可能会同时删除本地记录，请在删除应用前自行备份重要数据。

3. 胃安记可以替代医生诊断吗？
不可以。胃安记仅用于个人健康记录，不提供医学诊断或治疗建议。如有严重或持续症状，请及时咨询医生。

4. 如何反馈问题？
请通过邮箱联系我们，并尽量说明你的设备型号、系统版本、App 版本以及遇到的问题。

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Will WeiAnJi upload my data to a server?
No. By default, WeiAnJi stores user records locally on the device.

2. Will my data remain after I delete the app?
Deleting the app may also remove local records. Please back up important data before uninstalling the app.

3. Can WeiAnJi replace a doctor’s diagnosis?
No. WeiAnJi is only for personal health recording and does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment advice. If you have severe or persistent symptoms, please consult a doctor promptly.

4. How can I report a problem?
Please contact us by email and include your device model, system version, app version, and a description of the issue.
